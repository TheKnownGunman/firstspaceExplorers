The Explorer's Creed

Benjamin Mutebi, 2025 AD
Man was created to be fruitful, multiply and have dominion over everything on Earth. The men to whom this truth was revealed only saw a small part of the grand plan. The One who created didn’t stop on earth. The stars, His grander Gallery, are out there. Their beauty begs my eyes to look. I must see what my creator made above the firmament. The glitters that lighten up my sky call me with a deep attraction. All beauty must be seen. And the heavens are more beautiful than what my desires could ever conjure. They humble me in my insignificance. And yet they smirk to remind me that maybe I am the only one here. And I am that special. But as long as my eyes can see, I long to see every corner of my creator’s world. I will explore the ends of the earth and the ends of our galaxy. And what I haven’t completed my son surely will. “The zeal for your house shall consume me.” The zeal to see the majesty of your mind in creation, shall be the last thing I feel. For that zeal is mine every moment I am awake. I am here to see, and to see I shall do. Till the furthest end of the universe, the seed of Adam must see. So prepare your feet, oh monkeys of the Earth. We are going to where we belong, everywhere!!!
